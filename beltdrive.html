Belt Drive

Mechanical Design · Drawings · Working with Machine Shops · Fusion360

A system requires 5 powered axes. However, only 4 outputs are available on the motor controller. This project shows how motion can be transferred from one axis to another.

Mechanical Design

For this project, we are replacing one stepper motor on the gantry with a dual shaft stepper. Additionally, we are replacing an adjacent stepper motor in the same plane with a passive "cage". The two are linked with a timing belt, which allows for synchronous motion between two axes. Fusion360 was used to design machined parts the passive drive cage and mount for the tensioner. The timing belt pulleys, belt, and belt tensioner are off the shelf.
